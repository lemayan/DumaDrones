Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about our drone delivery service? Find answers to some of the most common inquiries below.

1. How does your drone delivery system work?

Our drones are equipped with GPS tracking and real-time communication systems, allowing them to deliver packages directly to your location with utmost precision. Simply place an order and select the drone delivery option.

2. What type of packages can I have delivered?

We deliver a wide range of items, including food, medical supplies, and other essential items. Our drones can carry packages up to a certain weight limit for a safe and efficient delivery experience.

3. Is drone delivery available in all areas?

Drone delivery is available in select urban and suburban areas. You can check availability for your location through our app or website before placing an order.

4. How long does delivery take?

Delivery times vary based on your location and package type. Most deliveries are completed within 30 to 60 minutes from the time the order is placed, ensuring quick service.

5. What happens if the weather is bad?

Our drones are equipped to handle mild weather conditions. However, in case of extreme weather such as heavy rain or strong winds, deliveries may be delayed for safety reasons. You will be notified of any changes to your delivery.
